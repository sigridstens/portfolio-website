An Ultimate Player's Manifesto: Mission Brochure

If the sport of Ultimate Frisbee brings to your mind kooky characters running around in costume and having more fun than you could even consider having sprinting after a 175 gram piece of plastic — then you aren't wrong — but you are missing part of the picture.

The Ultimate Player's Manifesto is a mission brochure created as a marketing campaign to appeal to the social concern common in the ultimate community. The Manifesto is a petition by The Ultimate Project, an online platform that connects people to the sport of Ultimate Frisbee.

The interactive design of this brochure facilitates a meditative exploration of the values that an ultimate player possesses. Through the user's experience opening the Manifesto, they are prompted to consider why they play and what they stand for.
