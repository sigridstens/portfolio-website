SEA to SAN : Pacific Coast bike tour journal

Role: Web and graphics designer

Visit original site.

Think back to your adolescent adventures by bike and the inexplicable freedom you felt pedaling around your neighborhood, propelled by destiny, which you would later term: youthful exuberance. Now multiply that feeling by 1,082 miles — down coastal highways careening around curves, mounting cliffs, carrying your life on your bike.

SEA to SAN is an online journal chronicling a month-long bike tour from Seattle, WA, to San Francisco, CA. A co-creation by my partner Kevin and me, the website follows our daily excitements, pains, and general whims conveying the joys and misadventures encountered when traveling by bicycle. SEA to SAN was born from the dust from our tires and the spotty internet connection we had en route.

The jaunty graphics and clean design construct a simple layout that frames the content, highlighting the stories from the road and increasing mileage down the coast.
