Farkle Scoreboard, mobile scorekeeeper

Role: UI and graphics designer

Whether your adventurous nature takes you to a brewery, a weeknight board game party, or a 1,000 mile bike trip, you won't want to be caught empty-handed. That is why you never leave the house without a set of dice for an impromptu farkle match. Now that you have downloaded Farkle Scoreboard on your phone, six dice and your wand are all you need to have a roaring good time.

Farkle Scoreboard is a mobile app, co-created by my partner, Kevin, and me to score and record our game stats, so we can keep track of exactly how many times I have defeated him. I designed the interface and graphics, while he breathed air into its binary lungs using Kotlin and XML.

The use of color and attention to human-centered design principles makes for a friendly and functional application that users will enjoy even if the odds are not in their favor.
